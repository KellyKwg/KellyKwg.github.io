PUZZLE SLIDE

CONCEPT

A shuffle puzzle game where one can play while bored. There are levels of difficulty, ranging from the amount of tiles needed to be moved. Once all pieces are in their correct spot you win the game.There are options to what picture you would like to have the puzzle on. There is a time limit of 1 minute for each game you play.

WIREFRAME

◽ Loading bar when first entering website
◽ Homepage with level options
◽ Click on level to begin game
◽ Screen will change when time runs out
